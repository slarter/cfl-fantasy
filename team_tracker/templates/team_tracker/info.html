General Information

This is the official website for the 2023 Lads CFL Fantasy League. Each of the nine Lads will belong to one fantasy team which is associated with a different CFL Team. When a CFL game is played, we track a list of stats for each team that played. Each recorded stat generates points for the corresponding fantasy team based on its point value The league standings are based on each fantasy team's cumulative points throughout the season. Heads up, the stats and point values may change throughout the season if there is an obvious advantage or disadvantage towards certain teams.
